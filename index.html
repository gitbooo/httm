What is Morocco Solidarity Hackathon?

Welcome to Morocco Solidarity Hackathon, a 2-days AI hackathon dedicated to harnessing the power of artificial intelligence in mitigating natural disasters risks. Hosted by Mila, this event brings together brilliant teams to tackle challenges that demonstrate their ingenuity and expertise in earthquake risk reduction.

Throughout this hackathon, participants will have the chance to acquire new skills, collaborate with fellow enthusiasts, and create innovative solutions that could have a profound impact on earthquake preparedness and response. Don't miss this incredible opportunity to be a part of shaping a safer future in earthquake-prone regions. Also, you will be contributing to a noble cause. Your participation directly supports the invaluable work of the La Croix-Rouge Canadienne and the Croissant-Rouge Marocain.

An overview of the consequences of the earthquake in Morocco

The recent earthquake in Morocco has had devastating consequences. As of the latest update from the Moroccan Ministry of the Interior, the death toll stands at 2,901, with over 5,530 injured. The quake, which measured 7 on the Richter scale, struck on Friday evening. Rescue operations are ongoing, as teams continue to search for potential survivors. Beyond the immediate human toll, the quake has had a significant impact on infrastructure. Many have been left homeless, and over 500 schools, particularly in rural areas, have been damaged. In these regions, logistics and infrastructure challenges are exacerbating the difficulties of relief and recovery efforts.

What?

This hackathon is going to be a hybrid mix between talks from leading experts in AI and natural disaster response mitigation and management, along with brainstorming sessions to create solutions with a real impact. The scope of this hackathon is broad, any subject that you see fits within the title of the Hackathon is welcome ! Here are some ideas that we thought of, regarding themes that could be discussed :

  • AI for natural disasters risks mitigation
  • AI for natural disaster forecasting (earthquakes, floods, weather forecasting..)
  • AI for humanitarian and relief logistics
  • AI for designing supply networks to support disaster relief
  • AI for optimal rescue resource distribution (helicopters/drones)
  • Dataset curation, e.g. satellite or drone images, data related to logistics or to seismic activity
